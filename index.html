Welcome to my Website

Hi! My name is Brandon Reno and I am currently a Computer Science graduate student at University of The Pacific. When applying for jobs I often find it hard to express who I am as a person through just my resume or cover letter. There is so much more to the best employees than just their qualifications for a position. To illustrate who I am as a person beyond my resume I created this site.
Content on this site will focus on who I am as a person in hopes of introducing myself in a personal light. My site is split up into three pages, About Me, Projects, and Resume. As could be assume the resume section is just a copy of my most current resume. This was included to re iterate myself as a candidate to whoever is viewing this site. About me will give my story from a yung age up until the present. This page will share traits that I feel define me, when they started shining and how they can transfer to the workforce. Finally my project page will give an organized view on projects that I have created throughout my undergraduate and graduate years in university. The purpose of this is to showcase projects that I am proud of in a deeper light. If you would like to contact me further please feel free to reach out to my email written in the top right of the navigation bar.

With that being said I hope you enjoy learning more about myself outside of a resume view. Also, feel free to connect with me on Linked In!
